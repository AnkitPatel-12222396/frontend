In frontend we can use operators in javascript.
1->for addition
#for increasing by one 
    num=num+1;
    num+=1;
    num++;
#for adding a integer var x 
    num = num+var; 
    num+=var;
2->for substaction
#for decreasing by one 
    num=num-1;
    num-=1;
    num--;
#for adding a integer var x 
    num = num-var; 
    num-=var;


Similarly,we can extend it use in multiplication and division.
